Step-by-step Overview

A concise summary of the key stages we follow to complete a trade safely and efficiently.

1. Initial Inquiry

Buyer submits requirements via our inquiry form. We acknowledge and assign a trading manager.

2. Verification & KYC

We verify counterparties (KYC/AML), check supplier credentials and request inspection certificates if needed.

3. Sampling & Inspection

Samples and third-party inspections (SGS, Intertek) arranged on request to confirm product specs before contracting.

4. Contract & Terms

We prepare proforma invoices, contracts and agree payment terms (LC, TT, DP, SBLC) and INCOTERMS.

5. Banking & Payment

We coordinate secure banking procedures, escrow or confirmed LCs where required to protect both parties.

6. Logistics & Shipping

Organize transport, customs clearance and provide full shipping documentation (B/L, COA, SDS).

7. Delivery & Handover

Final delivery, release of documents and confirmation of acceptance; follow-up support after sale.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical trade take?

Timing varies by product, inspection needs and shipping, but typical turnaround is 2–8 weeks from inquiry to delivery.

Can you help arrange LCs and bank guarantees?

Yes — we coordinate with banks experienced in trade finance to ensure secure payment instruments.

Do you provide inspection reports?

Yes — we can arrange third-party inspections and provide COAs, SGS/Intertek reports as required.
