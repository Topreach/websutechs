Trading Process

1

Initial Inquiry & NDA

Submit your requirements and sign a mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement to protect confidential information.

  • Buyer/Seller registration
  • Mutual NDA execution
  • Initial requirements assessment
2

Verification & Due Diligence

Comprehensive KYC/AML verification, document validation, and counterparty due diligence.

  • Company registration verification
  • Banking references check
  • Trade history validation
  • Compliance screening
3

Offer & Negotiation

Receive formal offers, negotiate terms, and finalize pricing and contract conditions.

  • Formal Corporate Offer (FCO)
  • Price and terms negotiation
  • Contract drafting
  • Payment terms finalization
4

Contract Execution

Sign Sales and Purchase Agreement (SPA) and proceed with payment instrument setup.

  • SPA signing
  • Payment instrument setup
  • Performance bond issuance
  • Escrow arrangement
5

Delivery & Settlement

Product delivery, inspection, documentation, and final payment settlement.

  • Shipping and logistics
  • Third-party inspection
  • Document presentation
  • Final payment release
