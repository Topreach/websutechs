Product Overview

Jet A-1 is a kerosene-based aviation turbine fuel used worldwide in commercial and military aircraft. It meets stringent international specifications for safety, performance, and reliability in all weather conditions.

Our Jet A-1 fuel is sourced from certified refineries and meets ASTM D1655 and DEF STAN 91-91 standards. With excellent cold weather performance and a freezing point of -47°C, this premium aviation fuel ensures safe and efficient operations for all types of aircraft.
