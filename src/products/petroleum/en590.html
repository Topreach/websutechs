Product Overview

EN590 is the European standard for automotive diesel fuel, specifying the physical properties that all automotive diesel fuel must meet if it is to be sold in the European Union and several other European countries.

Our EN590 10ppm Ultra Low Sulfur Diesel (ULSD) is sourced from certified refineries and meets the highest quality standards. With sulfur content not exceeding 10 parts per million, this premium diesel fuel ensures optimal engine performance and reduced emissions.
