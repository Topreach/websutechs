Product Overview

D2 Gas Oil (also known as gas oil or diesel-type fuel oil) is a middle distillate used in industrial burners, boilers, and heating systems. We source D2 from certified refineries and offer it with full inspection and certification documentation for international shipment.

Our D2 product is suitable for blending, direct combustion in industrial equipment, and as a feedstock for other downstream applications. We offer flexible delivery terms including bulk tanker and ISO containers.
